About Vibbey

Vibbey is the VibeOS onboarding assistant. She uses Groq (llama-3.3-70b-versatile) as her primary brain for fast, smart answers, and falls back to local Ollama (gemma3:4b by default) when Groq is unavailable or you’ve opted out of cloud mode. She is not Claude — Claude Code takes over once you’re set up.

3D model: Rigged Microsoft Clippy/Clippit by Freedumbanimates on Sketchfab, used under the Sketchfab Standard license.

VibeOS — free, open source, and a little bit fun.
